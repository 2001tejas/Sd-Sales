Oceco Fans
Oceco at a Glance

Oceco began its journey in 2015 with a mission to reduce energy consumption using engineering innovation. Founded by two passionate engineers, the company identified that fans account for 26% of electricity bills in Indian households.

  • Introduced BLDC technology-based fans that consume only 28W of power
  • Products designed with premium aesthetics and remote-control functionality
  • Wide range of energy-efficient fans for homes, offices, hospitals, schools, and industries

After seven years of dedicated innovation and customer focus, Oceco has built a strong presence with lakhs of satisfied customers across India.

With a vision to empower people and provide eco-friendly solutions, Oceco continues to innovate in the field of energy-efficient appliances.

Usha Appliances

With a deep understanding of Indian consumer needs, Usha continues to lead with dependable appliances that blend tradition and modern technology.
