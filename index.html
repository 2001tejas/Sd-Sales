About OCECO

OCECO is a rising Indian brand known for its energy-efficient BLDC ceiling fans. With a strong focus on innovation, OCECO combines stylish designs, powerful performance, and sustainability — making it a value-for-money choice for modern homes.

Energy Efficient Technology

OCECO’s BLDC fans consume up to 65% less power than traditional fans, helping you save on electricity bills while promoting a greener environment.

Smart Controls & Design

With remote control, swing mode, LED underlight, and sleek aesthetics, OCECO fans are made for convenience and style.

Key Benefits

  • Up to 65% electricity savings with BLDC motor.
  • Noiseless operation for a peaceful environment.
  • Higher airflow reaching all corners of the room.
  • Integrated LED underlight with brightness control.
  • Built for durability with reduced wear and tear.
  • Available in stylish designs (3, 5, and 6 blade models).
  • Supports sustainability and reduces carbon footprint.
